To-use:

ENSURE THAT UVICORN IS INSTALLED


CLI usage:
python3 Techsen_CLI.py --raag="raag name"
or python3 Techsen_CLI.py -r "raag name"


UI usage:
Point your terminal to the project directory.
Then, run "uvicorn server:app"
Open the i/p adress and port on your browser of choice(uvicorn provides a handy,clickable link too)
Select raag, hit Start Generation.
First few seconds just sing your reference Sa for the model to catch your shruti, once it starts playing notes start singing
Enjoy :)
