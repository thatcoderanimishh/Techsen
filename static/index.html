About us

Problem Statement

Many aspiring Hindustani vocalists and instrumentalists lack access to trained accompanists or background musicians. Practicing without a proper melodic and rhythmic context often limits growth, creativity, and confidence.

Solution

Techsen provides an AI-powered accompaniment tool that generates classical Hindustani background scores live while you sing or play. With just a mic and our platform, artists can practice with professional-quality support anytime, anywhere.
